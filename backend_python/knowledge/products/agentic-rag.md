**Tagline:** Sovereign AI. The next evolution of information retrieval.

## What It Is

Agentic RAG doesn't just find documents — it reasons through them, resolves contradictions, synthesises evidence across sources, and executes follow-up operations autonomously. Deploy on your own infrastructure for complete data sovereignty.

Traditional RAG finds text. Agentic RAG thinks. It builds multi-step retrieval plans, audits its own findings, resolves contradictions, and delivers answers grounded in evidence — not probability.

## Core Capabilities

**Dynamic Multi-Step Retrieval**
Constructs custom query hierarchies that fan out across vector stores, keyword indexes, and structured databases simultaneously — fetching and joining related information points that a single search would miss.

**Self-Critique and Verification**
After each retrieval pass, the agent audits its own findings for logical gaps, contradictions, or missing context — then re-queries to resolve discrepancies before constructing a final answer.

**Context Compression and Synthesis**
Condenses thousands of retrieved document chunks into dense, structured synthesis — surfacing only the highest-signal content relevant to the query, with full source attribution and confidence scores.

**Downstream Action Handlers**
Beyond answering questions, agents can trigger downstream operations: generate reports, send notifications, update records in your CRM, or initiate approval workflows — all within the same reasoning loop.
