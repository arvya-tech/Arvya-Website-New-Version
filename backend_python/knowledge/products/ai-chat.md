## Core Capabilities

**Strict RAG Integration**
Every response is anchored strictly to your validated internal knowledge base — contracts, handbooks, SOPs, and product docs. Hallucinations are structurally eliminated, not just filtered.

**Dynamic API Tool-Calling**
The assistant connects to your internal APIs, CRM, ticketing systems, and ERPs in real time. It can check order status, retrieve customer records, escalate tickets, and trigger approvals — all within the chat interface.

**Sovereign Air-Gapped Deployments**
Run entirely within your private data centre or VPC. No external API calls. No third-party model providers. Your conversations never leave your infrastructure boundary.

**Multilingual and Domain-Tuned**
Fine-tuned on your organisation's terminology, processes, and language. Supports rich, context-aware conversations in English, Hindi, and major Indian regional languages out of the box.
